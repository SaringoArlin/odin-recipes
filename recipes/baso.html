Mie Bakso

This mie bakso Indonesian meatball soup is quick and easy to make. Itâ€™s so simple you can even make this during the week after work. Serve with only the beef meatballs and broth for a light meal or make a complete meal with noodles and Asian vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Spring onions
  • Garlic
  • Garlic powder
  • Coriander roots
  • Salt
  • Baking powder
  • Tapioca flour
  • Egg white
  • Beef tock powder
  • Nutmeg
  • Ground beef
  • Vegetable oil
  • Asian shallot
  • Liquid
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Place the meatball ingredients except for the ground beef and tapioca flour in the food processor
  2. Process until it becomes a smooth paste.
    3. Transfer the paste from the blender into a large mixing bowl with the ground beef, and tapioca flour.
  3. Mix until well combined.
  4. Shaping The Meatballs
  5. Heat some oil in a large deep saucepan, add the onions and fry until golden and add the garlic and cook for a minute more.
  6. Add the liquid to the pot along with the sugar and fresh ginger. Bring to the boil, turn down the heat, cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Turn up the heat and carefully add the meatballs to the soup.
  8. The raw meatballs will sink to the bottom of the pot, and when cooked, will float to the top. When they have floated to the top, allow them to cook for a few more minutes.
  9. Before removing the meatballs from the soup, check that they cooked through by splitting one in half.
  10. Taste the soup for seasoning and adjust if necessary. You can either leave the meatballs
  11. in the soup and serve straight away or remove the meatballs and allow them to cool. To reheat, put the meatballs back into the hot soup to warm through.
